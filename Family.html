My Lovely Family


My family consist of my father, mother and older sister. My father is Mohamad Razali Bin Hamzah and he turns 55 this year.
He is a wonderful man as he never complain when we siblings ask for help. Besides, he love to spend his free time with us and went shopping together.

As for my mother, I call her Ibu and she is a lovely mother that I could have asked for. She loves to cook for our family and sometimes we spend time wit baking together in the kitchen.

Speaking of siblings, I only have one sibling that I can rely on as she is my one and only sister. Ever since I was a child she has been by my side and help me through my ups and downs.
Though we often fight with each other but our bond is getting stronger every single time and she has changed me from someone who rarely speak with everyone else to someone who is talkative and I love the changes.

Finally, there is me the youngest member of the family. I love my small and lovely family as they have always been my backbone and support me on all the things I want to do.
